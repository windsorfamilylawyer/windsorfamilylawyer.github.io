FAQ

Windsor & Ontario Family Law Questions

Can urgent family issues be addressed quickly?

Yes. Urgent concerns involving parenting, safety, or serious financial harm can require immediate legal action and fast motion strategy. Splane Roy LLP is prepared to act quickly when the situation demands it.

Do you accept Legal Aid certificates?

Yes. Legal Aid Ontario certificates are accepted for eligible family law matters. Intake review helps confirm certificate scope and next procedural steps — contact us to discuss your situation.

Do you only handle Windsor files?

No. Services cover Windsor, Essex County, and surrounding cities including Chatham-Kent, Sarnia, and London, while supporting broader Ontario family law matters across multiple court locations.

What should I prepare before my consultation?

Bring your key documents, court dates, major communications, and a concise timeline of events. The more complete your picture, the faster we can assess your matter and chart the right strategy.

Can support and parenting issues be handled together?

Often yes. Many family law files require integrated strategy across parenting, support, and property-related issues. Splane Roy LLP is equipped to manage complex, multi-issue files holistically.

Can you help with child protection / CAS files?

Yes. The service scope includes child protection matters, parenting litigation, and urgent family court strategy when children are at risk. Legal Aid certificates may be accepted for eligible CAS matters.
